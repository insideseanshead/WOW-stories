WOW Story from Touchmark at Mount Bachelor Village

Touchmark at Harwood Grove (TFND), prospective residents and neighbors John & Jo have been coming to Touchmark events for several years. Due to some changes in Jo's health, a majority of the cooking, cleaning, driving, and household chores were falling on John. The TFND sales team decided to surprise and delight them. They provided them with a food care package from the Touchmark kitchen courtesy of Ashley Thompson. The team delivered several days' worth of food as a basket, and John shared how their relationship began after John had delivered a May Day basket to Jo many years ago. Their mothers were in cahoots, and John's mom had called ahead to Jo's mom, letting her know John was on his way over with a May Day basket. A kiss was shared, and the rest is history!

The team received the following card from John and Jo: Melinda & Crew: The world is a little brighter thanks to you, Touchmark, for making our day very special. What great treats! The food was over the top. We enjoyed every morsel and our fun visit. Kudos to the chefs. No doubt the nicest May Basket we have ever received! John & Jo Lawrence
