What is a WOW story?

A WOW story is a specific example of an interaction or experience that shows the incredible lengths a team member has taken to solve a unique problem, delight someone, or go out of their way and typical job responsibilities in service of others. Examples can include planning a milestone celebration, after-hours help, volunteering extra help during a crisis or inclement weather, visiting someone in need off-campus, celebrating a legacy or life in a special way, etc.
